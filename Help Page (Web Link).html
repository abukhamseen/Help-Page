Not Sure How To Sign-In?

How do I get a BSK App account?

Once you’ve provided your email to the BSK registrar. You will be emailed a link to join the BSK App.

Once you have clicked the link in the email to join the application. A web page would appear where you would have to input a password you would like for your BSK App account.

Once the password has been chosen a confirmation email should appear in your account with your new login details.

Once the confirmation message appears in your account you can begin using the BSK App.

You can change your password at anytime in the Setting page on the BSK Application.

How do I sign in?

Once you’ve installed the BSK App on your phone or tablet; launch the application to make it to the Sign In page.

Enter your email associated with your account or the email provided to the BSK registrar. Then in the following field enter the password used when creating your account.

Once you’ve entered the details needed in the Sign In page you should be redirected to your dashboard.

What happens when I sign in?

To access your BSK account, you need to login from the BSK App on your phone or tablet. Once you're signed in, you can access the BSK App services.
